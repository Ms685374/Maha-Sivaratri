The Story of our Shivaratri

The Great Night of Shiva

Maha Shivaratri, the 'Great Night of Shiva,' is celebrated on the 14th night of the new moon during Krishna Paksha in the month of Phalgun. This sacred night commemorates the marriage of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, symbolizing the union of consciousness (Shiva) and energy (Shakti).

The Light That Guides Me

Just as Shiva appeared as an infinite pillar of light, guiding Vishnu and Brahma, you are that light in my life. When everything feels uncertain, you are my constant. You remind me that love is not about proving who is greater but about surrendering to something greater than ourselves

The Strength in My Heart

Like Shiva drinking the poison to save the universe, you have a strength that amazes me. You hold your struggles within, smiling as if nothing ever hurts. But I see you. I see the depth of your heart, the fire in your soul, and the love that flows effortlessly from you. You make me want to be better, to love better.

Sacred Traditions

🕯️

🌙 Fasting, Like I Fast for You

They say devotees fast on this night to purify themselves. If my heart could fast, it would fast for you, emptying out everything except love.

🪔

🌿 Night Vigil, Like the Nights I Stay Awake Thinking of You

Devotees stay up all night in worship. I stay up, lost in the thought of you, in the sound of your laughter, in the way your presence feels like home.

🙏

🔥 Abhishekam, Like How My Heart Pours for You

Shiva is worshiped with milk, honey, and devotion. My love for you is like that—pure, deep, and never-ending.

✨

🌸 Bilva Leaves, Like the Little Things That Matter

Just as Shiva loves Bilva leaves, I love the tiny moments with you—the way you talk, the way your eyes sparkle, the way you just exist so beautifully.

A Message for My Parvati, My Shakti

My Zubi,

Like the eternal love between Shiva and Parvati, my feelings for you transcend time and space. They say that when Shiva and Shakti unite, the whole universe finds balance. That’s what you are to me—you are my balance, my peace, my chaos, my everything.

On this night, when the world prays for love, strength, and clarity, I pray for only one thing—to always have you beside me, just like Parvati stood beside Shiva, unwavering, undying. Like Parvati meditated for years to be with Shiva, I will always choose you—again and again, in every lifetime. May Lord Shiva bless you with all the happiness in the world. May your heart always be as kind, your soul as fierce, and your dreams as limitless as the universe itself.

Thank you for being the Shakti to my Shiva, the energy that completes me and gives purpose to my existence.You are not just my love. You are my Shakti—the force that makes my world move. You are my Parvati—my eternal companion, my divine blessing.

With all my love,devotion forever and beyond, ♥ ♥
